Behind the Mask

Itch.io: Behind the Mask

Behind the Mask is a game jam project made in a group for Global Game Jam 2026 with a theme of "mask" and a time limit of 48 hours.

Behind the Mask was made with Unreal Engine 5.

In the game the you have to escape an old castle while hiding in the shadows. You can dodge the enemies, eliminate them stealthily or engage in combat which may lead to your demise more easily. You also have to find items that will help you to escape the castle. There are also powerups that boost your abilities.

What I did in the project:

Enemy design, programming and audio.

SFML/OPENGL Game Engine

This is a two-course school project where the goal was to create a game engine first in SFML and later in OpenGL. Our team had to develop three example games based on Arkanoid, Pac-Man, Donkey Kong and Tetris. I was assigned the Pac-Man clone.

Tools used for the project:

Visual Studio 2022

CMake 3.9

C++

Libraries:

SFML 2.6.0

OpenGL

I created a Pac-Man clone named KAMKman, the main features being:

-A playable character with similar movement to Pac-Man, including movement buffer for turning at intersections.

-Five enemy types with different AI:s, most of them having pathfinding.

-Four different powerups that give the player abilities such as eating the enemies or going through walls.

-Two maps that will loop with an increasing difficulty, the difficulty level affecting the enemies' speed and the duration of the powerups.

-Highscore system and customisable appearance.

Marine Descent

Marine Descent is a game jam project for Metroidvania Month 23 with the time limit of one month.

Marine Descent was made with Game Maker Studio.

Root Cause

Itch.io: Root Cause

Root Cause is a game jam project made in a group for Global Game Jam 2023 with a theme of "roots" and a time limit of 48 hours.

Root Cause was made with Unity 2021.

In the game you defeat waves of enemies and upgrade your abilities that will help you survive more easily. Your abilities include:

-A powerful punch you can perform with your "hand".

-A slingshot root that throws projectiles at the enemies.

-A bramble that has an aura that slow enemies down.

-An explosive acorn.

What I did in the project:

Graphics, programming and audio.

Scroll of Truth

Scroll of Truth was made with Game Maker Studio.

Scroll of Truth is a 2D action-platformer game where you have to beat eight different levels to obtain the titular 'scroll of truth'. Nocturnal Freelancer

Nocturnal Freelancer is being made with Game Maker Studio.

Nocturnal Freelancer is a 2D ghost hunting horror/puzzle game where you explore various haunted locations and try to figure out what kind of a ghost you're up against. You have tools such as an EMF reader, a spirit microphone and a smartphone and you can find other useful items around the locations too. You can upgrade your gear, character and apartment after you gain enough money and experience from the missions but getting caught by a ghost will be punished. In addition to figuring out the ghost type, you will have other randomly selected objectives as well. The more you complete, the more you progress.

Dead Channel
