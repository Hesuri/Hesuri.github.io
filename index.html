Behind the Mask

Itch.io: Behind the Mask

Behind the Mask is a game jam project made in a group for Global Game Jam 2026 with a theme of "mask" and a time limit of 48 hours.

Behind the Mask was made with Unreal Engine 5.

In the game the you have to escape an old castle while hiding in the shadows. You can dodge the enemies, eliminate them stealthily or engage in combat which may lead to your demise more easily. You also have to find items that will help you to escape the castle. There are also powerups that boost your abilities.

What I did in the project:

Enemy design, programming and audio.

SafetyVR

SafetyVR is a school project made for a simulation environments and educational games course.

SafetyVR was made with Unreal Engine 5 and Valve Index VR kit.

In the game you encounter several setups for possible workplace accidents in a VR environment. Your objective is to cause these accidents after which the game will tell you how these situations can be avoided so that a workplace can be safer for everyone.

Souls of Tuonela

Itch.io: Souls of Tuonela

Souls of Tuonela is a game jam project made in a group for Game Week Game Jam Kajaani 2024 with a theme of "Tuonela" and a time limit of 48 hours.

Souls of Tuonela was made with Unreal Engine 5.

In the game you have to find your way through Tuonela and shoot through hordes of swans, fiery souls and other enemies. You can find new weapons, upgrades to your weapons and other items that will help you to complete your journey.

What I did in the project:

Enemy design, programming and audio.
